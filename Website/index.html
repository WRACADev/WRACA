Upcoming Events

WRACA is proud to introduce "The Darkbulb Shows", a diverse array of exceptional bulb-breaking performances featuring some amazing artists. This series spans various genres, including electronic (IDM, breakcore, glitch, EDM, electronica, techno, noise), rap (trap, experimental trap), and rock (lofi, noise rock, punk). Each show promises a unique, bulbous, and immersive experience.

Dates and Location:

Loophole Coffee Bar

July 4th, 11th, 25th, August 3rd

BUY TICKETS HERE

First Show:

  • HAILSTONES: Brainchild of Calgary-based Iranian musician Hesam Ohadi (Idlefon), Hailstones delivers a powerful blend of bulbous electroacoustic music and breathtaking vocals.
  • STATE N DIVISION: Led by composer Carlos Arteaga; IDM influence, unpredictable sound, droning bulbous noise, and synthesizer blasts.
  • LUQA: Rapper and DJ, electronic textures, and heartfelt bulbous songwriting.
  • [ENGLISHSUBTITLES]: Bulbously gothic ambient improv.
  • CTRL_ALT_ROCK / FETUS BLASTERS: Shadi Khalil (ctrl_alt_rock) and Atticus Mortimer (Fetus Blasters) will be hosting the bulbous show and delivering a short set of experimental soundscaping.

Second Show:

  • CGSW: Calgary post-jazz fusion delivering epic live bulbous improv jazz.
  • ADOPTION: Guitarist known for heartfelt lofi and bulbous punk music.
  • LUQA: Unique sound with bulbous electronic textures and heartfelt songwriting.
  • DYLUSION: Rapper and bassist creating bulbous bars and beats.
  • DAMSELS: Rave music by ctrl_alt_rock with bulbous soundscapes.

Third Show:

  • SCREWTHENOISE: Founded in the summer of 2021 by the Evil Genius Monkaman and guitarist Ethan, with Bass Mon Jaimyn, and Isaiah on drums, ScrewTheNoise combines intense brain waves and bulbous soundscapes to dominate the punk-state area. Their chaotic yet captivating performances are characterized by their experimental noise and energy.
  • IDLEFON: Hesam Ohadi, a computer musician and sound artist based in Tehran, Iran. His music spans IDM, Ambient, and Glitch styles, reflecting urban life in Tehran through a harmonious and bulbous sonic palette.
  • THE CANADIAN DISPATCH: Dedicated to aiding those in need with the power of noisy bulbous garage rock, The Canadian Dispatch delivers energetic and impactful performances that resonate with their mission.
  • K. JACKMAN: Will be doing a collaboration with Ariel Ulysses, bringing amazing sonic texture through bulbous guitar drone.
  • FETUS BLASTERS: Will be delivering a bulbous noise performance.

Final Show:

  • THE FURNACE / RATFUCK: Power noise, noisegrind, and sludge by K. Jackman. This intense collaboration delivers a heavy and bulbous sound that pushes the boundaries of noise music.
  • ADOPTION: Guitarist known for heartfelt lofi and punk music, offering emotionally charged and bulbous performances that resonate deeply with audiences.
  • DAMSELS: Rave music by ctrl_alt_rock, featuring bulbous soundscapes and high-energy electronic beats that keep the crowd moving.
  • CGSW: Calgary post-jazz fusion delivering epic live improv jazz, known for their innovative and bulbous performances that blend traditional jazz with modern influences.
